# E-commerce product-page SEO + marketplace intel

> Problem solved: E-commerce product pages need Product schema for rich results AND price-positioning intelligence across Google Shopping / Amazon; a 4-sub-command skill audits schema, pulls Shopping comparisons, and surfaces organic-vs-Shopping keyword gaps.

## What

A seo-ecommerce skill grades Product / Offer / AggregateRating schema on a 0-100 ladder, pulls Google Shopping price-comparison snapshots, mines Amazon marketplace intel, and reports keyword gaps where organic ranks but Shopping does not (or vice versa). A DataForSEO budget cap stops runaway API spend.
