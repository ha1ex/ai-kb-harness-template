# Community-chat → daily/weekly musical-genre podcast summary bot

> Problem solved: active Telegram communities generate too much volume for members to keep up; static digests feel like a chore to read.

## What

A bot reads the last day/week of a Telegram chat, summarises it as a podcast script in a chosen musical genre (grime, dub, etc.), generates the audio via Suno and a matching stylistic image, then posts the bundle back into the chat. Functions as a community-building artifact ("a Ghibli-style growth hack") more than a knowledge tool — entertainment carries the engagement that summaries alone don't. Reusable for internal-comms in slack/discord channels.
