# Newsletter operating template

> Problem solved: Founder-run newsletters drift into "company updates"; a template enforces value-first content with retention benchmarks and UTM hygiene.

## What

A newsletter skill packs 6 subject-line formulas, a 60/25/15 section structure (value / curation / company), and a retention red-flag table that fires when open-rate drops or unsubscribe-rate climbs past defined thresholds.
