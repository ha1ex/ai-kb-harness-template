# Local-SEO maps intelligence

> Problem solved: Local businesses lose 10-40% of revenue tied to Google Maps visibility without geo-grid tracking, GBP audits, or name/address/phone consistency across map providers.

## What

A local-SEO skill runs a 3-tier geo-grid Share-of-Local-Voice scan, audits the Google Business Profile against a completeness ladder, cross-checks NAP consistency across Google / Bing Places / Apple Maps / OpenStreetMap (Overpass, Nominatim, Geoapify), and flags fake-review patterns. Citation-update aging follows an industry 18-day-rule heuristic.
