# Document automation across ERP + e-signature portal

> Invoice → contract → e-sign → back to CRM is hand-passed. End-to-end automation; QR-code on every invoice for one-tap re-pay.

## What

End-to-end document automation: CRM-triggered invoice / contract draft → push to an e-document workflow service → counterparty signs → signed PDF returns to CRM and to accounting. Specific concrete win: QR-code on every invoice so the customer can re-pay with one tap from the PDF. Pattern generalizes across CRM + ERP + e-document workflow combinations (e.g., DocuSign + NetSuite + HubSpot).
