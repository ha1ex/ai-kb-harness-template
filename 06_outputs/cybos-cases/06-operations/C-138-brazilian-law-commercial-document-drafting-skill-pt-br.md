# Brazilian-law commercial-document drafting skill (PT-BR)

> Problem solved: Brazilian founders signing PJ×PJ or PF×PJ engagements need PT-BR contracts with employment-link (vínculo empregatício / CLT) guardrails, MEI / Simples Nacional tax flags, and LGPD addendum templates.

## What

A PT-BR legal-drafting skill produces contracts, NDAs, and MSAs with a CLT vínculo-empregatício risk checklist, MEI / Simples Nacional / ISS / INSS tax-classification flags, IGP-M indexation clauses, CAMARB arbitration options, and an LGPD data-protection addendum. References Lei 9.609/98 and Lei 9.610/98 for software/IP clauses. Narrowly scoped to Brazilian law.
